$$
\begin{align*}
&\int \sin u \space du=-\cos u+C &\int& \csc u \space du=\ln{|\csc u-\cot u|}+C\\
&\int \cos u \space du=\sin u+C &\int& \sec u \space du=\ln{|\sec u+\tan u|}+C\\
&\int \tan u \space du=\begin{cases}-\ln{|\cos u|}+C\\\ln{|\sec u|}+C\end{cases} &\int& \cot u \space du=\begin{cases}-\ln{|\csc u|}+C\\\ln{|\sin u|}+C\end{cases}\\
\\
&\int \cos u \space du=\sin u+C &\int& -\csc u \cot u \space du=\csc u+C\\
&\int -\sin u \space du=\cos u+C&\int& \sec u \tan u \space du=\sec u+C\\
&\int \sec^{2} u \space du=\tan u+C&\int &-\csc^{2} u \space du=\cot u+C
\end{align*}
$$

### Inverse trigonometry
$$
\begin{align*}
&\int \frac{du}{\sqrt{n^{2}-u^{2}}}=\sin^{-1}\frac{u}{n}+C&-\\
&-&\int& \frac{du}{u\sqrt{u^{2}-n^{2}}}=\frac{1}{n}\sec^{-1}\frac{u}{n}+C\\
&\int \frac{du}{n^{2}+u^{2}}=\frac{1}{n}\tan^{-1}\frac{u}{n}+C&-
\end{align*}
$$

### Hyperbolic
$$
\begin{align*}
&\int \sinh u \space du= \cosh u+C\\
&\int \cosh u \space du=\sinh u+C
\end{align*}
$$

### Exponential
$$
\begin{align*}
&\int n^u\space du=\frac{n^u}{\ln n}+C\\
&\int e^u\space du=e^u+C
\end{align*}
$$

## Definite integrals
$a$ and $b$ are the lower and upper bounds, respectively.
$$
\begin{align*}
&\int_a^bf\ dx=F\bigg|_a^b=F(b)-F(a)\\
&\int_a^bf\ dx=-\int_b^af\ dx\\
&\int_a^af\ dx=0\\
&\int_a^b nf\ dx=n\int_a^b f\ dx\\
&\int_a^b(f\pm g)\ dx=\int_a^bf\ dx \pm \int_a^b g\ dx\\
&\int_a^b f\ dx=\int_a^d f\ dx+\int_d^b f\ dx\\
\end{align*}
$$
