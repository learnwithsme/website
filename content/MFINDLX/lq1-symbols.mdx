### Non-return valves

| Symbol | Name | Description
| --- | --- | ---
| <ImageInvertedColor src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/44/Symbol_Check_valve.svg" /> | Check valve | Air can move from left to right, but not in reverse.
| <ImageInvertedColor src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/c/c7/Symbol_Check_valve_%28spring-tensioned%29.svg" /> | Check valve (with spring) | Air can move from left to right, but not in reverse.
| <ImageInvertedColor src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/2/2c/Symbol_Shuttle_valve.svg" /> | Shuttle valve | OR gate.
| <ImageInvertedColor src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/1a/Symbol_Two_pressure_valve.svg" /> | Two pressure valve | AND gate.
| <ImageInvertedColor src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/64/Symbol_Throttle_valve.svg" /> | Throttle valve | Restricts air flow.
| <ImageInvertedColor src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/ea/Symbol_Throttle_valve_%28cross_section_setable%29.svg" /> | Throttle valve (adjustable) | Restricts air flow.
| <ImageInvertedColor src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/11/Symbol_Retract_resistor_check_valve.svg" className="h-14"/> | Two-way flow control valve | Air can freely go from left to right, but is restricted from right to left.<br/>(Depends on the direction of the check valve)
| <ImageInvertedColor src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/fe/Symbol_Quick_exhaust_valve.svg" className="h-14"/> | Quick exhaust valve | Air can freely go from left to top, but is exhausted from top to right.
