**Differentiation** is the process of finding the derivative of a function.


## Four-step rule

<div role="tablist" className="tabs tabs-lifted ">
  <input type="radio" name="my_tabs_2" role="tab" className="tab [--tab-bg:oklch(var(--b2))]" aria-label="Easy-to-understand definition" checked />

Given $f(x)$:

1. Solve $f(x+h)$
2. ↑ that $- f(x)$
3. ↑ that $÷ h$
4. All of ↑ that, take $\lim_{h\to 0}$

  </div>

  <input type="radio" name="my_tabs_2" role="tab" className="tab [--tab-bg:oklch(var(--b2))]" aria-label="Formal definition" />
  <div role="tabpanel" className="tab-content bg-base-200 border-base-300 rounded-box p-6">
  
Given $y=f(x)$:
1. Substitute $x$ with $x+\Delta x$ and $y$ with $y+\Delta y$.
2. Solve for $\Delta y$.
3. Divide both sides by $\Delta x$.
4. Find $\lim_{\Delta x\to 0}$ on both sides.
