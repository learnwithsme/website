In this subject, you will create a six-sided dice.

In order to create something that utilizes the machines and tools found in a machine workshop, you must know how to read engineering blueprints. The video below will briefly recap your learnings in engineering drawing.

`video:https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1Hm5Zyjmjac`

## Safety equipment
For the machine workshop, need the following safety equipment:
* Safety shoes (steel-toe)
* Safety goggles (especially for the drilling)
* Safety gloves (the one with a rubberized palm)
