MFGSEMS is a 10th term subject wherein you're required to plan, organize, and excecute the following:

* 7 workshops / cultural development
* 7 seminars

This may be done by visiting manufacturing plants and holding talks there.

## Planning

Ideally, this subject must be planned at least a year in advance. The entire batch must discuss what are the available options, on whether international or local, the budget, etc..
